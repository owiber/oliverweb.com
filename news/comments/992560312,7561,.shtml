I'm a bit bored... here's my sorta "line by line" interpretation:

"Desperate for changing, Starving for truth"
He wants to change and know the truth (God)

"I'm closer to where I started - I'm chasing after you"
He's becoming a stronger Christian, striving to be closer and closer to God ("chasing")

"I'm falling even more in love with you - Letting go of all I've held onto"
Falling more in love with God, letting go of the worldy/sinful things that hold him back from God.

"I'm standing here until you make me move"
He's "standing here" ready to do what God asks him to do.

"I'm hanging by a moment here with you"
I could come up with a couple different things for this one... but right now, I'm thinking it might be: he's holding on to life ("hanging") because of each "moment" he's with God.

"Forgetting all I'm lacking - Completely incomplete"
He's lacking and "incomplete" without God, but God completes him and makes him "[forget] all [he's] lacking".

"I'll take your invitation - You take all of me"
He accepts God into life - turns his life over to Him("take all of me").

...

"I'm living for the only thing I know"
He's living because he knows God and knows God loves him.

"I'm running and I'm not quite sure where to go And I don't know what I'm diving into"
He's following what God tells him, although he's not sure where exactly it will lead him in life and what it will lead him to do, or struggles he will face, etc.

"There's nothing else to lose
There's nothing else to find
There's nothing in the world
That could change my mind "
That's kinda self-explan. I guess... =)

The whole song could be viewed as a guy/girl thing as well, I could write that whole thing up there in that point of view... I just like it the way I view it more. =)
