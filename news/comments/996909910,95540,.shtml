My Poem Saturday, August 4, 2001
I am a spider
Just a lonely little spider
I live in a hole in the ground
I come out after it rains
I come out to see the rainbows
I come out for fun
But I'm a spider
We aren't supposed to have fun
So maybe I just come out to eat flies.
-Wes
