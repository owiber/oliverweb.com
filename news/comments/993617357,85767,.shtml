OK, oldest guy in the bible... was Methuselah who lived to 969 years old... how do you think he died? I would guess... Old age... but...

Genesis 5:26 "And all the days of Methuselah were nine hundred and sixty and nine years: and he died"

his son, Lemach was born when Methuselah was 187

Genesis 5:25 "And Methuselah lived a hundred eighty and seven years, and begat Lamech"

and then, Lemach had a son, Noah

Genesis 5:28-29 "And Lamech lived a hundred eighty and two years, and begat a son, (29) and he called his name Noah.

Genesis 7:6 "And Noah was six hundred years old when the flood of waters was upon the earth"

lala... 187+182+600 = 969... which means that Methuselah either died the year of the flood, or in the flood...
