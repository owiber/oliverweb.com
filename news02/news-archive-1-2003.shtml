Tolkien Monday, January 27 2003
Man, J.R.R. has to be one of the coolest dudes ever. I'm only half-way through LOTR, but I like it a lot. He just has a way of putting certain things into words (and by "words" I also mean the ones he made up =) to make it sound cool. I was talking to Mark and concluded that I would have very much liked to have hung out with Lewis and Tolkien (btw, what's with the initials? J.R.R, C.S.?) and smoked pipe with them in Oxford... even though that's probably what ended their lives.

Mithrander... man, that's just so COOL. I don't know why I like it so much, but I think Tolkien's languages are really really spiffy. I, for one, would have no objection to this guy actually creating a real language for people to speak... it'd be the language of coolness. =)

Three Rings for the Elven-kings under the sky,
Seven for the Dwarf-lords in their halls of stone,
Nine for the Mortal Men doomed to die,
One for the Dark Lord on his dark throne
In the Land of Mordor where the Shadows lie.
One Ring to Rule them all, One Ring to find them,
One Ring to bring them all and in the darkness bind them
In the Land of Mordor where the Shadows lie.

Argh, so cool!

I've decided my son's name is going to be Mithrandir...

Okay, fine... maybe just his middle name. ;)

-Oliver
