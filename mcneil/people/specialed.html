The Area 13 Special Olympics Basketball Tournament

    Thirty athletes from Round Rock ISD participated in the Area 13 Special Olympics Basketball Tournament in February (1997) at Southwest Texas State University. R. Anderson stated the Oath to the approximately 300 atheletes and spectators.

    McNeil winners were:

    C. Fryer -- 2nd place in Individual Skills
    S. Peterson & J. Hart -- 1st place in Team Skills
    M. Williams -- 2nd place in Team Skills
    M. Jennings, J. Suarez & Robyn Anderson -- 3rd place in Team Play Bracket Division
