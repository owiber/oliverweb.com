Dr. Joe Kopec
Dr. Joe Kopec was named principal of McNeil High School at the Board meeting May 18, 2000. Dr. Kopec has more than 20 years of campus administrative experience, including four years as principal and executive principal in Bryan, Texas, three years as principal of Austin High School in Austin, Texas and four years at San Marcos High School, San Marcos, Texas. He has also served as a campus administrator at the middle school level. Student performance improved during his tenure as principal at every school to which he has been appointed. Dr. Kopec’s other accomplishments include serving on the Board of Directors of the Texas Association of Secondary School Principals and the Texas Middle School Association and as chair of the High School Committee of the Texas Association of Secondary School Principals.
