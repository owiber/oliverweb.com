Lynne Johnson on Art:

"I love teaching high school art, expecially when I have students for more than one year. Since I have no children of my own it's rewarding for me to be a creative influence upon my students - sort of like being an 'art parent'.

As far as my own personal art is concerned - The photos on this page and the previous one are of a sculpture I recently completed. The sculpture is approximately 4 feet high. The medium is ceramic, wood and plexiglas. In the top, behind the plexiglas, is a light.

Last summer I went to Chichen Itza to see the Mayan pyramids. This sculpture has little ceramic tiles which represent the 20 days and corresponding planets of the Mayan calendar.

I often incorporate influences from ancient cultures into my art. I have a great deal of respect for these so-called 'primative' artists and feel that these cultures have much more wisdom than what present day man has given them credit for."
