Cultural Fair

The first annual McNeil Cultural Fair will be held on April 12, the first time ever at McNeil High School as a fair! A plethora of cultures will be represented at this first annual McNeil Cultural fair on the 12th of April. Performances and Booths will be set up in the main gyms throughout the day as well as a session during the evening for family to attend. Did I say the first annual McNeil Cultural Fair will be held on April 12th at McNeil High School?  Setting up of the fair is still a work in progress, but we will have it done by the 12th of April for the first annual McNeil Cultural Fair. If you so desire, attend the meetings, which are held on Wednesday afternoons in L100 to help us with this event.
