Oliver's "Christmas Carole" =)


Our story begins with Minix Scrooge in the small town of Round Rock.


As you can see, he was very mean and there was no one he didn't like to mock.
 

He always thought he was the best.

 

He enjoyed inflicting pain, his dad has confessed.

During Christmas times, when everyone was happy with glee...

Minix Scrooge would reply "Bah-humbuggy to thee!"
 

On Christmas Eve, Minix Scrooge set off to bed...

 

but was so tired, he fell asleep on the stairs instead.
