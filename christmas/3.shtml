Oliver's "Christmas Carole" =)


Late that night, when it was almost ten...
 

A ghost visited Minix's den.

"I am the Ghost of Christmas past!!!"
 
 

Minix quickly awoke as the Ghost exclaimed, "Let us visit one of your Christmas last!"
 
 

As the two departed into the sky,
 
 

Minix thought he was going to die.
 
 
 

Soon, the Ghost showed Minix himself when he was ten.

 

Minix soon said, "I looked like a moron then."
 

And the Ghost replied, "Not much has changed as you can see."
 
 

"Now you know you must change the ways that you will be."
 

Once again they lifted off into the air.
 
 

An instant later, Minix found himself in his lair.
