To the Barn!

So they left the North Pole, but this time with Oriana at the helm.

They got there rather quickly and arrived in Israel near Jerusalem.

They searched and searched, but could not find the one.

"Where is he?" they all thought as they sat down to eat a Cinnamon bun.

After eating, they began walking again, through a small town called Bethlehem.

On this small trip, Minix slammed into a large ram.

"Heh, whoops" he said as a stranger helped him to his feet.

"Sorry my ram got in your way, my name's Joseph, let me help with something to eat."

"Follow me to the barn, I've got some bread there, it's pretty doughy"

"What're your names? You can call me Joey."

To the barn we go!...
