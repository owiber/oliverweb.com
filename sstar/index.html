The McNeil High School SIGNING STARS is an organization of which any student in 9th, 10th, 11th, and 12th grades at McNeil can become a member. The basic requirement to become a SIGNING STAR is a solid commitment to remain chemical-free and be enrolled in at least ASL I (American Sign Language I).  SIGNING STARS organizes activities with deaf students in  RRISD, primarily McNeil High School, Deerpark Middle School and Live Oak Elementary School, and sponsors Silent Dinners once per month at local restaurants and Close-Captioned Movies.  SIGNING STARS also participates in other community service activities, including but not limited to, working with the students from Texas School for the Deaf.  

The SIGNING STARS provides its members with a structured activity and club in which they can participate and produce/support setting healthy, realistic personal goals.  The commitment required by SIGNING STARS fosters healthy lifestyles for both participants and recipients.  The SIGNING STARS take pride in their participation and representation; they strive for good grades and behavior and like being positive role models for their peers and younger students. SIGNING STARS  provides students with a positive group in which to belong and in turn, provides a vital service within the school system and community.

If you have any questions about SIGNING STARS, contact Terry Sheneman at 464-6320.
