Introduction
 

What is YouthWAVE?

At YouthWAVE you can expect to experience a strategic and proven evangelism training experience for high school students. Give us one week, we'll give you a vision and plan for the other 51 weeks of the year. Our Sharing training walks through the specifics of a Gospel presentation; Responding training helps students move their friends past spiritual sticking points; Living training challenges students to a lifestyle of relational evangelism.

When is YouthWAVE?

Saturday, June 21—28, 2003

Where is YouthWAVE?

Concordia University – Austin, Texas
www.concordia.edu

Who do I bring to YouthWAVE?

YouthWAVE is for committed Christians who are serious about sharing their faith with their friends and are ready for an intense week of training that will stretch and challenge them.  Returning students have the option (with their youth leader’s permission) of attending the Impact My World track.  This track prepares those who have mastered Track 1 of YouthWAVE to become the spiritual pacesetters in their world by following Christ’s example and strategy for ministry.

Where does YouthWAVE fit?

YouthWAVE is delivered in the context of Christ’s strategy for making disciples. We strongly recommend that each Group Coordinator attend a Sonlife Youth Strategy Seminar (see http://sonlife.com/events/index.asp for dates in your area) prior to YouthWAVE if possible.  YouthWAVE is a proven ministry tool that will sharpen the effectiveness of your youth ministry—regardless of the size of your group.
