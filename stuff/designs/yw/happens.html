What Happens There?
 

What is Taught

The training presented at YouthWAVE has been designed and developed over a period of 20 years for the purpose of "equipping students to share Christ with their friends."

Two tracks are provided for students. The "Impact My Peers" track is for first year participants. The "Impact My World" track is for YouthWAVE alumni who have implemented the first year material into their lives.  This track requires their Group Coordinator to affirm that this person is truly applying what they have learned.

Impact My Peers

Responding
Addresses the issues concerning WHY Christians believe what they believe.

 

  • Session One - Diagnosing the Spiritual Roadblocks
  • Session Two & Three - Dismantling the Spiritual Roadblocks
  • Session Four - Directing Toward a Decision
  • Session Four - Depending on God

 

Sharing
Addresses the issues concerning WHAT Christians believe.

 

  • Session One - Starting a Spiritual conversation.
  • Session Two - Sharing the Gospel
  • Session Three - Stopping the presentation of the Gospel
  • Session Four - Success & Styles (How to "Go and Sow")

 

Living
Addresses "How to" of group and individual outreach

  • Session One - Purpose and Process
  • Session Two - Whole Group Outreach Events
  • Session Three - Small Group Outreach Events
  • Session Four - Personal Outreach

 

Impact My World (YouthWAVE Alumni Track)

Witnessing Experienced

Students take the practical training and do four days of witnessing in strategic areas in Austin.  Students are paired up with other team members.

Concerts of Prayer

Each day while witnessing is taking place some groups are designated to stay behind and pray for the groups who are witnessing.  Concerts of Prayer our times set aside to take students through a season of prayer for the Groups, the lost peers of students and for themselves.

Large Group Celebrations

After each breakfast and lunch time students will be led through a time of praise and worship.  Each evening there is a time of celebration to thank God for the blessings of that day and give back to Him all the glory and honor due Him.

Large Outreach Event

Rather then just tell you how to do a large outreach event we will show you how to do one.  We want you to be able to have a vision on how to do one in your own town.

Letter Writing

You will spend a afternoon writing letters to the peers you had chosen before camp.  You will be led through the experience of writing a letter based upon where you lost friend is in their spiritual life.  These letters are then brought together and prayed over and then sent out.  Your friends in some cases will have these letters before you return home.

Youth Group Time

Unlike many camps YouthWAVE is designed to prepare students to serve in their local church.  Though witnessing and class time are experienced in selected groups, all other time is spent in your youth group.  Our hope and prayer and our goal is to have everything transferable to your home congregation.
