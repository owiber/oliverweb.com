What's the difference between Liquid PM and Sunday?
In short – the time Saturday 7pm and a different band. The speaker will be the same. Talks will be the same. We will be releasing a public beta of “tribes”.

Isn’t Liquid PM a seeker service?
Both Sunday and Saturday are seeker services. Both Sunday and Saturday are worship services. (*Remember we’re all seekers. We don’t know everything there is to know about God that we can say we’re no longer seeking Him.) The same talk will be given. It’s mainly a time difference. We want your friends to be comfortable with whichever time slot fits best for them.

Why should I make the switch to Liquid PM?
Our entire purpose really is about making it easier for your friends to find a spiritual home. If we’re honest about it, 90% of college students wake up on Sunday at noon the earliest. So 11:00am is NOT an accessible time. When making the choice, the main question is – when would my friends go? If that’s Saturday, go Saturday. If it’s Sunday, then stay on Sunday. The question is no longer – “when would Liquid best fit me?” Look at your friends, and you’ll see where you should go.

Can I go to Sunday worship and when my friends want to come, can I go on Saturdays?
Yes, but we do not recommend it. We want you to go the time slot your friends would go, regardless if they come any given week. It will be important that you invite them into community with other Liquidians when you go – those being the same Liquidians that you worship with week after week.
