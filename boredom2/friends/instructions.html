INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Using the java applet, deform the face as much as you want.
  2. When you are done, hit the Print Screen key on your keyboard.
  3. Open Paint (you can use other image programs if you have another one, but for this, I'm just going to explain how to do in in MS Paint)
  4. Go to Edit=>Paste
  5. Select the "Select" option from the sidebar (the one that looks like a dotted lined box)
  6. Using the "Select" tool, select the part you want to send to me.
  7. Go to Edit=>Copy
  8. Go to File=>New
  9. Go to Edit=>Paste and place the image at the top left of the white space.
  10. Deselect your picture (right click) on the image.
  11. At the bottom right corner of the image, you should see a tiny blue square, click and drag this to get rid of all the extra white space.
  12. Save the pic and send it to me! (oliver@tiberiumsun.com) =)
